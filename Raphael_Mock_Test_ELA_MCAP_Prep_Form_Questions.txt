1. What is the main topic of the passage about Jazz music?

   ○ The history and elements of jazz music
   ○ Famous jazz musicians from New Orleans
   ○ How to prepare for MCAP tests
   CORRECT: The history and elements of jazz music

2. What does 'improvisation' most likely mean in the context of jazz music?
   ○ Playing music from written sheet music exactly
   ○ Creating music spontaneously while performing
   ○ Listening to jazz recordings quietly
   ○ Studying music theory in a textbook
   CORRECT: Creating music spontaneously while performing

3. According to the passage, where did Jazz originate?
   ○ New York City
   ○ Chicago, Illinois
   ○ African American communities in New Orleans
   ○ European concert halls
   CORRECT: African American communities in New Orleans

4. Why might studying Jazz help students with their math skills?
   ○ Jazz musicians are usually good at math
   ○ Jazz involves rhythm patterns and fractions in time signatures
   ○ Students calculate how much music costs
   ○ Jazz bands need to count their instruments
   CORRECT: Jazz involves rhythm patterns and fractions in time signatures

5. How does the passage organize its information about Jazz?
   ○ Problem and solution
   ○ Cause and effect
   ○ Description of a topic with supporting details
   ○ Chronological order of events
   CORRECT: Description of a topic with supporting details

6. The students were ______ to begin their new unit on Jazz music.
   ○ bored and uninterested
   ○ very enthusiastic and eager
   ○ slightly nervous
   ○ feeling tired
   CORRECT: very enthusiastic and eager

7. Jazz combines elements of blues, ragtime, and ______ structure.
   ○ related to harmony and chords in music
   ○ very loud and noisy
   ○ slow and boring
   ○ only for dancing
   CORRECT: related to harmony and chords in music

8. Students would listen to recordings from different jazz ______.
   ○ time periods or generations
   ○ types of musical instruments
   ○ volumes or loudness levels
   ○ music stores or shops
   CORRECT: time periods or generations

9. As part of their unit, students would ______ the rich history.
   ○ ignore or avoid
   ○ travel to a different country
   ○ investigate or study carefully
   ○ forget completely
   CORRECT: investigate or study carefully

10. The unit would connect to their studies in history, culture, and mathematics.
    ○ separate from
    ○ be related to or linked with
    ○ replace completely
    ○ have nothing to do with
    CORRECT: be related to or linked with

11. Which sentence is written correctly?
    ○ The students is excited about jazz.
    ○ The students are excited about jazz.
    ○ The students am excited about jazz.
    ○ The students be excited about jazz.
    CORRECT: The students are excited about jazz.

12. Which word correctly completes the sentence: 'Ms. McClure posted the homework assignment ___ the class website.'
    ○ in
    ○ on
    ○ at
    ○ by
    CORRECT: on

13. Which sentence has correct punctuation?
    ○ After lunch we went to the library to study
    ○ After lunch, we went to the library to study
    ○ After lunch we went, to the library to study
    ○ After lunch we went to the library, to study
    CORRECT: After lunch, we went to the library to study

14. Which is the correctly spelled word?
    ○ recieve
    ○ receive
    ○ recieve
    ○ receeve
    CORRECT: receive

15. Which words should be capitalized in this sentence: 'we studied jazz music in mrs. mcclure's class on monday'
    ○ we, studied, jazz
    ○ Jazz, Mrs. McClure's, Monday
    ○ We, Jazz, Monday
    ○ We, Jazz, Mrs. McClure's, Monday
    CORRECT: We, Jazz, Mrs. McClure's, Monday
